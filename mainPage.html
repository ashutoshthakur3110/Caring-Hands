Making a difference, One meal at a time

Welcome to Daanpatra, a food donation center dedicates to helping the less fortunate. We provide and arrange food for those in need, while collection surplus food from hotels and events.


The Problem

The challenge is not a lack of food - it is making food consistently available to everyone who needs it.

Hunger kills more people each year than AIDS, malaria and terrorism combined

Every 10 seconds, a child dies from hunger

82% of hunger people live in countries with food surpluses, not food shortages

One in every eight people sleeps hunger each night

One third of the food produced around the world is never consumed

850 million hungry people in the world

On the group's first night of distribution, we realized that helping the less fortunate may feel good personally, but feeding 50 odd people at night, once a week would not create any real difference in a country where millions are starving.
